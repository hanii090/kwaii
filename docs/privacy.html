Privacy Policy

Kawaii Meds

← Back to Kawaii Meds

Effective Date: February 16, 2026

Kawaii Meds ("we", "our", "the app") is a medication reminder app with a virtual pet companion. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

All app data is stored locally on your device using on-device storage (AsyncStorage). This includes:

  • Your display name (optional, entered during onboarding)
  • Medication names, dosages, and schedules
  • Virtual pet progress (level, XP, coins, streak)
  • Notification preferences
  • Medication history (last 90 days)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly back up your device through your operating system's backup features (e.g., iCloud, Google Backup).

2. Notifications

Kawaii Meds uses local notifications to remind you about medications. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to any server. You can disable notifications at any time in the app's settings or your device's system settings.

3. In-App Purchases

Kawaii Meds offers an optional one-time premium purchase ("Kawaii Premium") processed through Apple's App Store or Google Play Store. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple or Google — we never see or store your payment information.

We use RevenueCat to manage purchase entitlements. RevenueCat may receive an anonymous app user ID to verify your purchase status. No personally identifiable information is shared with RevenueCat. Please refer to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details on their data handling.

4. Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not collect usage statistics, crash reports, or behavioral data. There are no third-party trackers in the app.

5. Children's Privacy

Kawaii Meds is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided data through the app, please contact us.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by going to Settings → Clear All Data within the app. This permanently removes all locally stored data including your profile, medications, pet progress, and history. Alternatively, uninstalling the app removes all local data.

7. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is:

  • RevenueCat — for in-app purchase management (their privacy policy)

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
